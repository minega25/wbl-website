OUR COMPANY BACKGROUND

We’re Carefully Delivering your happiness.

Established in 2006, World Business Logistics Ltd. is Rwanda's leading logistics company specializing in the customs clearing and forwarding, transportation, and warehousing. We provide customers in Rwanda, and worldwide with expedited air, ground and ocean heavyweight services, reverse logistics, warehousing and specialised disribution, custom brokerage and compliance and logistics services.
